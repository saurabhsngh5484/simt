About Sun Institute of Management and Technology

Sun Institute of management and Technology is an integral part of simt Institutions, which is a name synonymous with student empowerment through excellence in higher education. Sun Institute of management and Technology, started in 2008, is affiliated to mjpru University, Bareilly and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and has NBA accredited courses.
